Michael Scott Simpson, Esq.

Partner

Michael Simpson has been practicing law in the State of Colorado for over 20 years. During this time he has concentrated his practice in exclusively representing defendants in insurances disputes. Mr. Simpson has conducted numerous jury and bench trials around the state of Colorado. He has extensive experience in handling auto liability litigation, bad faith insurance disputes, DUI and Dram Shop litigation, wrongful death and premises liability disputes. Mr. Simpson regularly handles high profile DUI and Wrongful Death cases and has achieved recognition for substantial expertise in Colorado Bad Faith litigation.
