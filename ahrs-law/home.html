Founded in 1969, our firm is a Denver-based law practice representing clients in civil litigation cases and specializing in insurance defense disputes throughout the state of Colorado. Based upon our substantial and successful legal experience, we not only aggressively represent our clients in the trial and appellate courts in Colorado, we continually seek new and innovative ways to achieve expeditious resolution of disputes through negotiation and mediation. We have a proven track record in handling large complex litigation but also the difficult to resolve disputes of all sizes in an efficient and cost-effective manner.
