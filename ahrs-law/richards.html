Jeffrey J. Richards, Esq.

Partner

Jeffrey Richards has practiced law in the State of Colorado for over 25 years, concentrating his practice in the area of civil litigation. He has tried over 50 jury trials in courts around the State. Mr. Richards has focused his practice in the defense of automobile liability, dram shop, and civil DUI litigation, premises liability, insurance bad faith, ski accidents and professional negligence involving pharmacists. Over the years, he has handled high profile cases which have included wrongful death actions and major infectious disease food contamination litigation.
