International

Jeffrey Richards is active in Colorado’s international legal and diplomatic community. He is the Honorary Consul of France for Colorado, and a member as well as the past Dean of the Colorado Consular Corps., Colorado’s principle diplomatic organization.

Mr. Richards’ international experience includes being a White House, Executive Office of the President, Intern/Clerk in the Office of the U.S. Trade Representative and working for the International Monetary Fund in Washington, D.C. Mr. Richards has worked in Paris, France for an international clothier and as a translator at the Université René Descartes, Paris V.

Mr. Richards is a fluent French-speaking U.S. attorney. He advises clients with interests in both Colorado and France regarding civil and business matters that involve the two countries. This office handles certified, legal translations for all types of U.S. and French documents.
