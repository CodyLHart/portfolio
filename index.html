ABOUT ME

Having spent the past ten years pursuing a number of different creative endeavors, from touring the country as a rock and roll guitarist to starting an absurdist apparel company, graphic design and web development have always been an infrequent side hustle for me. Having spent a number of years doing small web design projects for bands, artists, and lawyers I developed a deep love of CSS that I just can't seem to kick. Whenever other developers ask me what CSS framework I used on a project, I am always proud when I can say that I didn't. Eventually I worked to develop a deeper relationship with my second love, JavaScript, and I knew that I needed to be focusing a significant portion of my time and energy on continuing to develop my programming abilities. Throw in a few more languages and some more experience with the back end, and I have worked to become a more confident programmer than I ever would have imagined.
